Darong Glass Bottles is committed to providing comprehensive explanations and guidance on the implications of various Incoterms, helping you make informed decisions.We clarify the responsibilities and risks associated with common Incoterms, including:EXW (Ex Works): Under EXW, the buyer assumes all costs and risks from the moment the goods are made available at the seller's factory or warehouse.FOB (Free On Board): The seller is responsible for delivering the goods on board a vessel nominated by the buyer at the specified port of shipment, with risk transferring at that point.CIF (Cost, Insurance and Freight): The seller covers the cost of goods, freight, and insurance to bring the goods to the named port of destination, though risk transfers once the goods are loaded onto the vessel.DDP (Delivered Duty Paid): This term places maximum responsibility on the seller, who bears all costs and risks, including duties and taxes, until the goods are delivered to the named place in the buyer's country.Our primary goal is to help you select the most appropriate logistics model tailored to your specific needs.
